About

I'm a Computer Science Master's student at Ontario Tech University, where my research focuses on human motion generation. I completed my Honours BSc in Computer Science with a Data Science specialization and a Math minor, graduating with a 4.2/4.3 GPA.

My work centres on machine learning and computer vision. I enjoy building projects end-to-end, from data pipelines and model architecture to deployable machine learning solutions. I've placed at national hackathons and taught programming to students of all ages along the way.

VCLab Shaz-NET

🏆 2nd Place - Ottawa Defense Tech Hackathon

A fast, modular acoustic ML platform that identifies and detects drones in real time using only a laptop microphone. Built an audio pipeline on log-Mel spectrograms feeding CNN- and transformer-based classifiers, reaching ~95% accuracy on the evaluation set, with a clean UI showing color-coded temporal activity.

Human Action Prediction - Undergraduate Thesis

Apr 2025

Skeleton-based action prediction using knowledge transfer. Designed a deep learning framework that classifies partially observed actions by transferring knowledge from a full-action recognition model, improving early-prediction accuracy. Trained and evaluated on N-UCLA's Multiview Action 3D dataset.

  • PyTorch
  • GCN
  • TCN
